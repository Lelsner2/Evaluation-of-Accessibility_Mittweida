About the Evaluation

Report Creator
Leonie Elsner
Evaluation Commissioner
Not provided
Evaluation date
Mon Oct 28 2024 - Fri Nov 8 2024

Executive Summary

The website is based on the TYPO3 v8.7 CMS. Some content appears to be created by people with very limited knowledge of HTML and CSS, especially for the pages which concern topics not part of the city's immediate jurisdiction, like the page for the city's swimming pool.

Important to note is that there are also several pages which have not been included in the report due to them being either under construction (https://gemeinsam.mittweida.de/, http://www1.mittweida.de/live/) as of the writing of this report, or are not managed/used directly by the city and/or are simply hosted by them (Bibliothek, Amt24).

Scope of the Evaluation

Website name
Public Website of the city of Mittweida

All Web Content of the public website of Mittweida. Located at https://www.mittweida.de Specifically the following address: https://buergerbeteiligung.sachsen.de/portal/mittweida/beteiligung/themen/1028685

Additional evaluation requirements
The report will include a description of the problem and reasons for them. Due to the report being done manually without automated tools, some errors might not have been detected. Due to the amount of PDFs found amongst the pages, they are not being included in the evaluation, this exclusion also affects PDF forms.
